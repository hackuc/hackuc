FAQ

How much is HackUC going to cost?

HackUC is completely, 100% free. We want to spread Computer Science Education, and Education should always be free. Come to learn and have free food!

I don't know how to code, should I still come?

Of course you should come! HackUC aims to teach everyone more about Computer Programming, even if you haven't coded a single thing in your life. Our goal is to have you leave with a working application that you build yourself! Check this out for more info!

What should I bring?

Bring your laptop, chargers, power strips (if you can), school ID, and an attitude to learn! We'll be providing everything else, just bring anything you need to feel comfortable over 12 hours.

I think I know someone who would be willing to sponsor, is that ok?

Of course that's ok! Let them know to email us at sponsor@hackuc.com and we'll get back to them as soon as possible.

I'm not a student, but I want to help out!

Perfect! HackUC always needs more mentors, and if you're able to help anyone out please contact us at contact@hackuc.com and we'll get back to you as soon as possible! We need all the help we can get, so no matter what your specialty, please help out!

I have more questions!

We knew you'd ask this, for any other questions that weren't answered, feel free to email contact@hackuc.com and we'll get back to you as soon as we can.
