Meet the Team

Jonathan Shih Executive Director

I first became involved in the hackathon through the public relations side of it-while my peers used their coding expertise to build the hacking component of the hackathon, I reached out to firms, hoping they would see the value of the high school hackathons fueling the minds of future employees. Now, as Executive Director, I hope to bring computer science to everyone, regardless of their background. Outside of the hackathon, I’m a student teacher and spokesperson in the Taiwanese community, former intern at an e-commerce startup, class treasurer, Makerspace Executive President, FBLA Vice President of communications, but above all a tired high school student.
